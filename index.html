The Hidden Problem

Sector-Focused Trading Is Leaving Quality Compounders Behind

Public markets are increasingly dominated by trading teams with rigid sector mandates. If your company doesn't fit neatly into their predefined boxes—Healthcare, Tech, Industrials—you become invisible to the capital that should be finding you.

Your fundamentals are excellent. Your growth trajectory is proven. But because you span multiple sectors or defy easy categorization, you're trapped in a valuation discount that has nothing to do with your actual performance.

This problem is accelerating. As sector-specialized trading continues to dominate, the penalty for being a "multi-industry" or "diversified" company only grows.
